1. Membrane Diffusive Transfer

Feasibility: 4 | Novelty: 2 | Reg: 2 | Timeline: 3-5yr
This IS current ECCO2R
Ideas:
• Microfluidic CO2 stripper chip (Okafor)
• Vacuum-assisted membrane extraction (O'Brien)
• Countercurrent dialysate membrane (Nair)
• Heat-exchanger CO2 transfer (O'Brien)
• Bubble aeration for CO2 release (Marco)

Key advantage: Proven tech, clear regulatory path
Key limitation: Incremental over existing ECCO2R

2. Sorbent / Chemical Capture

Feasibility: 4 | Novelty: 3 | Reg: 3 | Timeline: 3-5yr
Cartridge-based Regenerable
Ideas:
• Zeolite/amine sorbent cartridge (Chen)
• Ion-exchange resin for HCO3- (Volkov)
• Immobilized catalyst bed (Osei)
• Breathing circuit CO2 scrubber (Patel)

Key advantage: Selective, compact, regenerable
Key limitation: Blood biocompatibility of materials

3. Enzymatic / Metabolic Shift

Feasibility: 2 | Novelty: 5 | Reg: 5 | Timeline: 7-10yr
Highest novelty Highest risk
Ideas:
• Injected engineered carbonic anhydrase (Osei)
• Pharmacological Bohr effect enhancement (Yamamoto)
• CA reverse-reaction inhibitor (Osei)
• Enzymatic acceleration of HCO3- → CO2 (Chen)

Key advantage: Elegant, no hardware in blood
Key limitation: IND pathway, off-target effects, years of preclinical

4. Electrochemical Removal

Feasibility: 3 | Novelty: 4 | Reg: 4 | Timeline: 5-7yr
No consumables Selective
Ideas:
• Electrodialysis to drive HCO3- across membrane (Volkov)
• DC voltage selective ion removal
• Electrochemical CO2 conversion

Key advantage: Regenerates electrically, no sorbent replacement
Key limitation: Must prove safe current density in blood contact, hemolysis risk

5. Implantable / Distributed

Feasibility: 2 | Novelty: 5 | Reg: 5 | Timeline: 10+yr
Moonshot
Ideas:
• Subcutaneous "artificial gill" (Okafor)
• In-body high-surface-area membrane
• Vascular-integrated CO2 exchanger

Key advantage: No extracorporeal circuit at all
Key limitation: Unproven in humans, PMA, years of animal data needed

Baseline comparison
Ideas:
• Optimized MV / dead-space mgmt (Reyes)
• Inline ET tube CO2 filter (Reyes)
